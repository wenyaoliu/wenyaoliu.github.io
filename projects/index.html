Peridynamics-based simulation of asphalt mixture cracking in semi-circular bending (SCB) test

 1. Peridynamics model was applied to simulate SCB testing for asphalt mixtures. 2. Cracking patterns in simulation match lab observations well. 3. Fracture energy and P-u curve from simulation agrees with experimental results. 4. Arch effect and energy transformation during SCB testing were investigated.
